These creatures are formidable, but they are not invincible. You will not face them alone.”

With a sharp whistle, she summons a pack of great wolves, their silver coats shimmering like moonlight. “They will guide and help you,” she says, placing a hand on your shoulder. “But the fight is yours. Prove yourself again, and these lands will be free.”
